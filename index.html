How to Play

Use the following keys:

Right Move right
Left Move left
Up Fly
T Throw

Collect crystals to throw them on your enemies.

Collect heart-shaped apples to gain back lifes.

Be aware of the wraiths: Move out of their way or use your crystals.

After having survived all wraiths, you will come to the big demon. Make sure to have enough crystals left to fight her.
